    06 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Ashley Benson has left her fans in awe after sharing new photos of herself on her Instagram account where the blonde bombshell has over 21 million followers. Styled by Jacqueline Zenere, Ashley Benson wore the Cult Gaia Camila top and the matching Cult Gaia Cleo pants and looked magnificent. She paired the outfit with layers of gold jewelry and wore a pair of Cult Gaia heels for a polished, finished look. Ashley had worn her hair a darker shade of auburn and brown during her relationship with Cara Delevingne, but since their split and her new relationship with G-Eazy, she has returned back to her blonde locks.

    The Camille top is in the color ash and costs approximately $328. The top is made from 100 percent, faux leather, and zips in the back. The front top and bottom feature a pleated style that makes the bustier give the wearer an hourglass figure. With a slight flare at the bottom edge, the bustier top enhances the waist and looks sensational.

    Ashley paired the top with the matching Cleo Pant in the same ash color. The pants cost approximately $458 and are also made from 100 percent faux leather. The pants feature pleats at the waist that complement the pleats of the top.

    The high-rise pants reach the ankle and Ashley wore the matching Cult Gaia Sandra heels that cost $418. The heel is 4 inches in height, making Ashley Benson’s 5’3″ frame 5’7″. The beautiful blonde looked gorgeous as she posed for multiple shots and showed off her fabulous outfit. You may see several photos that celebrity stylist Jacqueline Zenere shared on her official Instagram account below.
