    DMX‘s family has recently addressed rumours which involve masters, merch and funeral. Check out the post that The Shade Room shared in order to learn all the available details.

    ‘DMX’s family shut down floating rumours regarding the late rapper’s masters, finances and merchandise, according to TMZ, cited by TSR.

    TSR also noted that ‘The family addressed a now-viral tweet that claimed #JayZ and #Beyoncé allegedly planned to purchase and regift DMX’s masters to his children. They expressed that the masters “aren’t in play” with the stars. Although #DefJam has some masters under control, they are not owned directly by Jay-Z,’ TSR says, citing info from TMZ.
