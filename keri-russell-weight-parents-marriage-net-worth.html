    Keri Russell is an American actress and dancer who has been captivating audiences with her versatile performances for over three decades.

    She is best known for playing the title role in the drama series Felicity, which won her a Golden Globe Award, and Elizabeth Jennings in the spy thriller series The Americans, which earned her several Emmy and Golden Globe nominations.

    She has also appeared in many films, such as Mission: Impossible III, Waitress, Dawn of the Planet of the Apes, and Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker.

    Currently, she is starring in the political drama series The Diplomat on Netflix, where she plays an American diplomat in London who gets involved in a dangerous conspiracy.
