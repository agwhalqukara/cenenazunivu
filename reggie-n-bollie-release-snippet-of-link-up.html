    Reggie 'N' Bollie Release Snippet of 'Link Up'

    Rap duo Reggie N Bollie stole the hearts of the nation during their time on the X Factor last year where they were mentored into second place by judge Cheryl, and now the lovable and energetic pair are gearing up for the release of their second single Link Up.

    21 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Rap duo Reggie ‘N’ Bollie stole the hearts of the nation during their time on the X Factor last year where they were mentored into second place by judge Cheryl, and now the lovable and energetic pair are gearing up for the release of their second single ‘Link Up’.

    They announced the news via their official Twitter account earlier this week and confirmed that the track will be released on Friday, December 9th.

    ‘Link Up’ which is the boys’ second single is the follow up to their hit debut release ‘New Girl‘ which dropped earlier this year.

    Following the exciting announcement about their second single being released this month, Reggie ‘N’ Bollie have now shared a short clip of ‘Link Up’ as a gift to their “true fans” on Twitter.

    #5DaysToGo ?We couldn't resist sharing it with You our true fans ??????? #LinkUp #DanceHallThing pic.twitter.com/86aEX9FUKU

    — Reggie n Bollie (@ReggieNBollie) December 4, 2016

    The snippet only lasts for 15-seconds but it is long enough to realise that the song is going to be an absolute banger and a massive club hit.
