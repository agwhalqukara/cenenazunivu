    Get To Know The Wedding Veil Unveiled Star Paolo Bernardini!

    Hallmark Channel is back with another installment of The Wedding Veil trilogy. Starring Autumn Reeser and Paolo Bernardini, the second movie follows Emma to Italy, where while teaching about American art, researches the provenance of their wedding veil said to bring its owner love.

    02 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Hallmark Channel is back with another installment of The Wedding Veil trilogy.

    Starring Autumn Reeser and Paolo Bernardini, the second movie follows Emma to Italy, where while teaching about American art, researches the provenance of their wedding veil said to bring its owner love.

    Paolo will star as Paolo, the son of a local lace-making family, that she meets in Venice.

    Click inside to learn more about Paolo!

    The Wedding Veil Unveiled is Paolo‘s first Hallmark movie. Previously, the 40-year-old Italian star has been seen in All The Money in the World, and In Search of Fellini.

    “Autumn, my travel companion, you are simply amazing! Thanks for everything,” he wrote in promotion of the film on his Instagram.

    Here are 5 Fast Facts about Paolo that you should know:

    • He’s a doting uncle! Paolo has featured two of his nieces on his Instagram and gushes over them a lot.
    • He likes to celebrate his birthday with a cupcake! You can see his most recent post here.
    • He started his career as a Kids’ Choice Award host! Paolo co-hosted the Italian version of the award show in 2008, 2009 and 2010.
    • He’s a world traveler. You can see a lot of his adventures on his Instagram, which can cure your wanderlust.
    • Paolo is a huge animal love. His Instagram is dotted with selfies with animals of all kinds.

    The Wedding Veil Unveiled will premiere TONIGHT, February 12 at 8 p.m. on Hallmark Channel.
