    If you’re a YouTube fan then you’re going to love HelloWorld Live! We’ve put together a handy guide containing all you need to know about the upcoming event.

    What is HelloWorld Live and when is it on?

    HelloWorld Live has been described as an ‘immersive live experience’ with a group of YouTube stars coming together for a four hour show. It will take place on October 28th and 29th at the Genting Arena, Birmingham.

    Who’s on the line up?

    The line up so far includes KSI, PointlessBlog, Zoella, Joe Sugg, Caspar Lee, Louise Pentland, Jim Chapman, Marcus Butler and Rose and Rosie. There are still many more guests to be announced for the event.

    HelloWorld Live Tickets

    There are three ticket types:

    VIP (standing) – This includes a meet and greet with the guests, a tour around ‘main street’ by the stars themselves and some exclusive tour goodies.

    Full Day (standing) – This gives viewers the opportunity to experience ‘main street’ on the ground floor prior to the live show.

    Stage show only (seated) – This is seated tickets for the live show beginning at 4pm.

    Tickets are available now on See Tickets and The Ticket Factory. Have you grabbed yours? They’re selling fast!
