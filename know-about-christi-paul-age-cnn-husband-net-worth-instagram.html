    After facing the emotional abuse from her former husband, Christi Paul decided to write a book so that the victims would find courage to seek the help. The award-winning journalist is relishing a family moment as a mother of three children with her now husband.

    Christi Paul is an American journalist currently serving as an anchor on CNN New Day, a weekday morning television show on the network. Besides, she appears as a weekday news anchor on HLN channel.

    Christi Paul Bio; Parents, Education, Height

    Christi was born on 1st January 1969 to parents Rojan Donald Paul and Ann Elizabeth. She grew up with her younger brother in Bellevue, Ohio.

    Standing at the height of 5 feet 3 inches (1.60 meters), Paul went to the University of Toledo in Ohio. There she earned her bachelor’s degree in Broadcast Journalism.

    Her Career; CNN, HLN, Net Worth

    Christi started her career as a news anchor, editor, photographer, and reporter at CBS-affiliated WDTV. Moreover, she worked as an anchor, reporter, and producer for NBC affiliate KTVB. She was a news anchor and weathercaster for ABC affiliate KNXV in Phoenix.

    In 2003, Paul joined HLN and now serves as a weekday news anchor on the network. Similarly, she appears on CNN as an anchor on a weekday morning television show, CNN New Day.

    Christi Paul anchors a weekday morning show, New Day on CNN (Picture: Pinterest)

    The CNN talent amasses her net worth through the earnings from television media. She is likely to earn a six-figure salary based on her decade of experience on CNN.

    Shares Three Children With Second Husband

    The 50-year-old currently shares the marital relationship with Czech husband Peter Paul. She married Peter at the age of 33 on 22 June 2003 at a beach in Kauai. The couple relishes family life as parents of three children. They have three daughters; Ava, Sofie, and Bianca. Paul often shares her family moments through her Instagram amassing more than 15k followers.

    Christi Paul celebrates her 50th birthday with her husband Peter Paul and three daughters on 1st January 2019 (Picture: Christi’s Instagram)

    Before Peter, Christi was married to reporter and news anchor Rob Koebe. However, the ended their marriage after getting divorced.

    Some Interesting Facts About Christi Paul

    • Christi won Miss Greater Cleveland in 1991 and was third runner-up at Miss Ohio in 1993.
    • She works on the National Advisory Council for the One Love Foundation in honor of Yeardley Love.
    • She published a book, Love Isn’t Supposed to Hurt in June 2013. The book comprises of the emotional abuse and violence from her first husband.
