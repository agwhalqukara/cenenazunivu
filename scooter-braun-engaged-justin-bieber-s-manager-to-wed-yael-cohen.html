    She said “Yes!” Scooter Braun and Yael Cohen are getting married, Us Weekly can confirm. Multiple sources tell Us Justin Bieber‘s longtime manager and his girlfriend got engaged Sunday, Jan. 5 at his home in Los Angeles.

    Hinting at the joyous news, the talent manager, 32, took to his Twitter Monday and shared with fans, “Loving 2014. Luckiest guy in the world.” Earlier that morning, he alluded to “a great night” and shared a close-up of a painting by Yorkshire-born artist Harland Miller named Incurable Romantic Seeks Dirty Filthy Whore.

    Cohen similarly alluded to the nuptials on Monday with a simple Instagram picture that read “Yes” in blazing red, cursive letters. (Braun’s new fiancee is the CEO of F–k Cancer, an organization that she founded after her mother was diagnosed with breast cancer in 2009.)

    “He is really happy” one source tells Us Weekly. Another insider adds of the newly-engaged couple, “They are head over heels and are so great together.”
