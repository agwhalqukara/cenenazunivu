    Daughter poses standing on her dad's grave, Mzansi is disturbed

    - In a weird turn of events, a woman has gone viral after posing for pictures whilst on her father's grave - @DarlingtonWrld was reposted by popular Twitter user @AdvoBarryRoux as she struck two dashing poses upon her father's grave

    - @DarlingtonWrld was reposted by popular Twitter user @AdvoBarryRoux as she struck two dashing poses upon her father's grave

    - The images have left Mzansi tweeps scratching their heads in confusion at her choice of location

    We all love a good selfie opportunity, whether it be in front of good scenery or we're just looking good. One South African woman has taken it to a new level as she posed on top of her father's grave.

    Well known parody account, @AdvoBarryRoux, posted up two screenshots by Twitter user @DarlingtonWrld in which she strikes two dashing poses standing on her own father's grave. She captioned it "Rest in Peace Dad, I will always remember you."

    The tweet has gained over 700 likes over 100 retweets since its upload. The replies section is full of concerned and disappointed Saffa tweeps who want to know why she would do something like this.
