    ‘You’ Storyline:

    ‘You’ is about a troubled man Joe Goldberg, who happens to be a serial killer. While Joe manages a New York bookstore, he develops toxic and extreme affection toward a customer Guinevere Beck. Subsequently, as his obsession grows for Beck, Joe becomes more desperate. As a result of this, Joe will do anything to put himself in the life of Beck, no matter the cost.
