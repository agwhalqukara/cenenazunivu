    As we announced earlier this week, Hayley Williams and Chad Gilbert attended their wedding ceremony and reception at a local restaurant in Franklin, Tennessee this weekend.

    A few days ago there weren’t any official posts from the couple, but now there is!  Chad took to Instagram to post the two photos below.

    Chad captioned this Instagram photo: “We Deeeeeed it! Photo by @davidbean“And this one with: “I wish I could live my wedding over again. Best day of my life. Thanks @AHeritageOfModernism for making my Groomsman and I look so fly.”

    Not using Instagram for 95 weeks now, Hayley Williams took to Facebook instead to show how happy she was after marrying the love of her life.  She posted the same first photo Chad did (as posted above) with the caption, “HELLO FROM THE OTHER SIIIIIDE! – The Gilberts (insert camera emoji) David Bean Photography !!!“

    With both rockers posting links to David Bean’s Instagram and Facebook account in their captioned photos, to give credit where it was due, one thing led to another and well… we found the rest of the photos David posted of the adorable couple!

    On David Bean’s Instagram, he posted the photo below.

    He captioned this Instagram photo: “One of my favorite photos I took of Hayley and Chad’s wedding this past weekend. You can see more at: http://www.people.com/article/hayley-williams-marries-chad-gilbert-wedding-details“

    After clicking the link in the caption David posted on the previous photo, these are the gorgeous photos we discovered.
