    Big Sean Net Worth

    Big Sean, an American rapper and singer whose real name is Sean Michael Leonard Anderson, has a net worth of $35 million as of 2023.

    He showed an interest in music from a young age and would participate in rap battles.

    Raised by his grandmother and mother, Big Sean’s talent caught the attention of Kanye West during a radio interview in his hometown. West even gave him some bars to use for a rap.

    Career

    Big Sean started rapping in high school and gained recognition through local rap battles. He caught the attention of Kanye West, who later signed him to GOOD Music.

    His debut album “Finally Famous” produced three hit singles and earned platinum certification.

    Sean released several successful albums like “Hall of Fame,” “Dark Sky Paradise,” and “I Decided.” He has also collaborated with many popular artists throughout his career and has a clothing company called Aura Gold.

    Big Sean Net Worth and Early Life

    Sean Michael Leonard Anderson, known professionally as Big Sean, was born on March 25, 1988 in Santa Monica, California.

    However, he moved to Detroit, Michigan at the age of three months and was raised by his mother and grandmother.

    His grandmother had served in the Army during World War II.

    In his music, when Big Sean mentions the “west side,” he is actually referring to the west side of Detroit rather than California.

    He attended Cass Technical High School and graduated with a 3.7 GPA.

    Personal Life

    Big Sean dated Naya Rivera, the actress and singer from “Glee,” for over a year after they met on Twitter.

    They became engaged in October 2013 but broke up in April 2014.

    He also had an eight-month relationship with Ariana Grande. From 2016 to 2019, Big Sean dated the singer Jhene Aiko.

    On August 4, 2011, Big Sean was arrested for third-degree sexual assault at a concert in New York.

    He pleaded guilty to second-degree unlawful imprisonment in October of that year and was fined $750.

    The charges of sexual assault were dropped as part of his plea bargain.

    Accolades

    Big Sean received four Grammy nominations, including Best Rap Song, Best Rap Performance, Best Rap Collaboration (with Kanye West and John Legend), and Album of the Year.

    He also earned six MTV Video Music Award nominations and won two awards for Best Video with a Social Message (for One Man Can Change the World) and Best Fight Against the System.

    Additionally, he received multiple BET award nominations and has won seven, including Best New Artist in 2012.

    Big Sean Net Worth and Real Estate

    In December 2017, Big Sean bought an 11,000 square foot home in Mulholland Estates for $8.7 million.

    The exclusive gated community is located above Sherman Oaks, California and was originally listed for $11 million.

    Slash, the Guns N’ Roses guitarist, was the previous owner of the house. In August 2021, Sean sold the property for $11.1 million.

    Recently, in October 2021, Big Sean purchased a new home in Encino, California for just over $8 million.

    The house is located near his former home, on the other side of the hill.
